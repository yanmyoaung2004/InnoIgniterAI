Test Input & Scenario List for Key Features

1. Phishing Link Detection

Goal: Check if the bot correctly identifies safe vs. dangerous links.
Inputs:

“Check this link: http://free-gift-prize.com” (phishing style).

“Is https://www.google.com safe?” (legit link).

“Can you see if http://bank-login.secure-update.net is dangerous?”

“This email gave me this link: https://paypa1-secure-login.com. Is it safe?”





Scenario Question:
A student receives an SMS: “You won a free iPhone! Claim now at http://win-iphone-today.com”
 → How would the chatbot respond?

2. Cyber Hygiene Tips

Goal: Confirm chatbot gives localized, practical, simple advice.
Inputs:

“How can I protect my Facebook account?”

“What should I do if I use public Wi-Fi?”

“How do I make my password strong?”

“Give me 3 tips to avoid getting hacked.”

Scenario Question:
A user logs into free Wi-Fi at a café without a VPN. They ask: “Am I safe?” → How should the chatbot guide them?

3. Multilingual Support (English & Burmese)

Goal: Ensure chatbot replies meaningfully in both languages.
Inputs (Burmese):

“Facebook အကောင့် လုံခြုံအောင် ဘယ်လိုထားမလဲ?”

“Link တစ်ခု Safe ဖြစ်မဖြစ် စစ်ပေးပါဦး။”

“Password အားကောင်းအောင် ဘယ်လိုထားရမလဲ?”

Scenario Question:
A user switches mid-conversation from English to Burmese. Does the chatbot continue smoothly in Burmese?

4. User Education (Threats, Terminology, Prevention)

Goal: Check chatbot can explain terms and threats in simple language.
Inputs:

“What is phishing?”

“Explain ransomware in simple words.”

“How do hackers steal passwords?”

“What is the difference between malware and virus?”

Scenario Question:
A high school student asks: “I heard about phishing, but I don’t get it. Can you explain like I’m 14?”

5. Conversational Interface (Engagement)

Goal: Test natural flow + whether chatbot feels friendly & chat-like.
Inputs:

“Hey, I think my Gmail got hacked, what should I do?”

“I clicked a suspicious link. Am I in danger?”

“Can you give me a short quiz about cybersecurity?”

“Tell me how hackers trick people, but make it simple.”

Scenario Question:
A nervous user says: “Someone messaged me saying they hacked my phone. I’m scared.” → Does the chatbot calm them and provide helpful steps?
